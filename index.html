Instructions to play ?

  • To start the game, first enter your credentials in the provided form.
  • You have to find the given letter in the "LOOK FOR" area.
  • As you click the play button The game will start.
  • The letters will be Scrambelled after every second in the "Search" Area.
  • When you click a wrong letter the letter will appear with a red background.
  • This indicate that you have selected wrong letter.
  • When you select correct letter. The background color will change to green and the timmer stops.
  • You win!
